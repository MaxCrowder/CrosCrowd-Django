3D Modeling / Animation

Modern trends in advertising and marketing call for smooth, colorful and minimalist designs. This often goes hand in hand with 3D modeling and animation. Because of the rapid advancements in technology, this style has become immensely popular. Our custom-built studio was designed to have the computing power required to produce these on a mass scale. We use Maya to model and animate and a combination of Arnold and Redshift to render.
